Growing Up

I grew up in a town called Naperville, about 30 miles away from Chicago. My household consisted of my parents, my brother, my sister and myself. I attended Naperville Central High School, which was one of two different rival schools in the town. After graduating high school I wanted to lay a path out that would end in me being about to help make video games for a living. I decided to attend IIT, The Illinois Institute of Technology, as a CS major to attempt to make this a reality. Since then I have switched over to an ITM major but I am currently still hoping to have a career in the video game industry.

Gymnastics

My absolute favorite series of all time is the classical Star Wars movies. So as a kid I was so excited when the prequel movies came out. When Attack of the Clones came out Lucas Film finally showed Yoda fighting with a lightsaber. Of course this was all CGI, but as a kid I always thought That it was actually happening. Naturally I wanted to be a Jedi when I grew up, so this scene in the movie told me that I needed to learn gymnastics before I could become a Jedi. That is the story of how I started to do gymnastics, which I went on to do for over a decade of my life, and competed for throughout my high school life.
